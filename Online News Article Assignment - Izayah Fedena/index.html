Fire in York County

Beginning

Firefighters were called to a home in Elham Drive at 6:04 am after a fire started in the area.
It took roughly 75 Firefighters about 30 minutes to get the blaze under control.

Source of Fire

The fire was said to have been started by a lightning bolt from a passing storm. The fire began in the attic before spreading throughout the entire household.

Conclusions

Crews estimate $225,000 in damages. Luckily, there were no injuries from the fire.
